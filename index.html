Welcome to VideoSaver.

VideoSaver is an iOS app that can download 'mp4' format video directly from Safari share extension, or you can save the link of video to the host app and download it later. The app contains custom player that can loop video, which is really useful for playing short video (like GIF). It supports background download, save video in both app cahce folder and user's Photo Library.
